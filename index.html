02 — Logo System

The crest is a map.
Five symbols, one lineage.

The Emwani crest reads top-to-bottom as a single sentence: royalty, identity, life, shelter, seed. Every variant — full crest, simplified, monochrome, app icon — preserves that vertical reading.

①

Green crest shield

The official Emwani crest is rendered in deep green (#1F5A3E). Crown peaks at the top, faceted body, and a pointed base — drawn from the uploaded reference. Never re-tint to gold; the official mark is always green.

②

Central coffee bean

A vertical coffee bean sits at the optical centre of the shield, in a slightly lighter green with a curved seam. The seed is the heart of the mark and never altered.

③

Faceted interior seams

Light-green seams radiate from the bottom point and arc across the top, evoking shelter, weave, and the Mushonge silhouette without illustrating it literally.

④

EMWANÍ wordmark — red serif

Set in a sharp red serif (#B43A2B) directly below the crest. The acute accent on the Í is part of the mark — preserve it. Never substitute another typeface.

⑤

Café variant

For café touchpoints only: add the word CAFE in green sans-serif, tracked, below the wordmark. Never use the café lockup on bean / wholesale / corporate surfaces.

Spacing & placement

Minimum clear-space = the bean

Around every crest, reserve a margin equal to the height of the central coffee bean. Nothing — type, photograph, edge, ribbon — may enter that area.

  • • Minimum size — crest: 32px / 8mm
  • • Minimum size — wordmark: 96px / 22mm
  • • Never tilt, never gradient-fill, never outline-only the bean
  • • Never place on photography without a ≥40% solid overlay

05 — Visual Motifs

The language of shelter,
leaf, and ceremony.

Six reusable motifs distilled from the profile. Use them as backgrounds, dividers, ornaments — never as decoration alone. Each motif has a meaning; deploy it where the meaning fits.

Mushonge — hut arcs

Background pattern at 8–14% opacity for heritage sections. Symbol of home, shelter, lineage.

Mushonge — dot weave

Lighter alternate. Excellent for cards, side panels, packaging belly bands.

Coffee leaves

Life, fertility, ethical cultivation. Use as frame ornament around portraits or in section dividers.

Coffee cherries

Harvest moments, "from the cherry" copy, seasonal campaigns.

Heritage B&W

Black-and-white treatment for portraits, archive imagery, founder story.

Red ribbon · gold wave

Section transitions. Red for ceremony, gold for warmth. Stack them or use solo.

Steam / pour

Hero footers, café sections, the "cup" moment of the bean-to-cup arc.

Farmer basket

Sustainability and sourcing sections. Always paired with named regions (Mbinga, Mbozi, Karagwe).

08 — Motion Style

Slow. Cinematic.
Like steam, never like a spring.

Motion at Emwani should feel like an opening credit sequence. No bounce, no overshoot, no spring physics. Long durations (800–1400ms), gentle easings, and a deep respect for mobile performance.

The seed
remembers.

Cinematic reveals

Headlines fade up over 1200ms · cubic-bezier(.22,.61,.36,1). One element at a time, never staggered like a chorus line.

Steam-like fades

Section-to-section transitions fade through a 30% black veil for 480ms. Like a film cut, not a slide.

Bean orbit

Hero loading state: three beans orbit the crest at 9 / 12 / 14 second cycles. Linear easing — the rhythm of a wheel, not a spring.

Packaging tilt

On hover, product pouches rotate ±8° on the Y-axis over 600ms. Never a flip. Never a spin. The product is sacred.

SCROLL · CHAPTER 03 / 05

Scroll-driven origin journey

The bean-to-cup arc unfolds with scroll position. A thin gold progress line in the margin marks the chapter.

DO NOT
Bounce. Overshoot.
Wobble. Auto-play loud video.
DO
Fade. Reveal.
Settle. Respect reduced-motion.

Mobile performance

No more than 2 simultaneous animations on mobile. Honour prefers-reduced-motion. Lazy-load every hero video below 720p.
