INGOMA
ROOTED IN
MBOZI
Awaken the Warrior in You
O2
From the red soils of Mbozi, where the
mornings rise with fog and the drums of
tradition still echo through the hills, Ingoma
– The Warrior was born. Inspired by the
ceremonial Ingoma dance of the Nyiha
people, this coffee honors the legacy of
men who led with rhythm, protected with
pride, and stood grounded like baobab
trees.
Ingoma is not just a performance. It is a
pulse — a heartbeat passed from father to
son, from soil to bean, from bean to brew.
This coffee carries that same masculine
strength. It is bold, deep, and unfiltered in
its truth. Cultivated at high altitudes,
roasted in small batches, and packed with
reverence, Ingoma – The Warrior is crafted
for those who rise early, lead boldly, and
carry generations on their shoulders.
