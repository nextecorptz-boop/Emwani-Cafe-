Emwani means coffee in the Haya language. From the highlands of Mbinga, Mbozi, and Karagwe — a Tanzanian house, returning African coffee to its rightful place at the world's table.

From the slopes of Mbinga,

01
Mbinga · Cherry

Hand-picked at peak

Smallholder cooperatives harvest only the ripest cherries. Each lot is named, dated, and traced back to the farm.

02
Karagwe · Mill

Wet-processed & sun-dried

The Kachebonaho-built mills strip, wash, and slow-dry each parchment on raised beds — the way the elders kept it.

03
Dar es Salaam · Roast

Slow, ceremony-led roast

Small batches. Profiles named for the ceremonies they honour — Chioda for harvest, Ingoma for the warrior.
